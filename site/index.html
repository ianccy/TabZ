View on GitHub

Features

Everything you need to keep your browser organized.

📂

Collections

Group saved tabs into named collections with custom colors and emoji icons. Keep related tabs together, always one click away.

🔀

Drag & Drop

Reorder collections and tabs by dragging. Move tabs between collections effortlessly.

☁️

Google Drive Sync

Sign in with Google to sync your collections across devices via Google Drive. Your data stays in your own Drive.

🔖

Linked Bookmarks

Link existing Chrome bookmark folders as live collections. Changes sync both ways automatically.

🌙

Dark & Light Mode

Toggle between dark and light themes. Customize your background with any color or image.

🔍

Search

Instantly search across all collections and open tabs. Find any saved page in seconds.
